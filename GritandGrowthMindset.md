# Grit and Growth Mindset – Assignment Answers

## Question 1: Grit
**Ans:** Grit means that intelligence or talent alone is not enough to succeed. True success comes from having a growth mindset and staying committed with passion and perseverance, even when things get hard or you fail.

## Question 2: Growth Mindset
**Ans:** A growth mindset means believing that I can improve my skills with effort. I focus on learning and getting better, not just on results, and I keep trying even when things are hard.

## Question 3: Internal Locus of Control
**Ans:** Internal locus of control means believing that what I do determines my results. When I take responsibility for outcomes, I stay motivated and continue to improve.

## Question 4: Key points to build Growth Mindset
**Ans:**
- I believe I can find a solution.
- I question my assumptions, both positive and negative.
- I respect the struggle and see it as part of learning and growth.

## Question 5: My ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset
**Ans:**
- I believe I can learn new skills and improve.
- I focus on progress, not just results.
- I put in regular effort and keep going even when it’s hard.
- I don’t let failures discourage me or take them personally.
- I accept feedback and use it to get better.
- I take responsibility for my actions and believe I am in control of my outcomes.
