## Question 4:
**Paraphrase the Sleep is your Superpower video in your own words in brief. Only the points, no explanation.**

**Ans:**
- Sleep is very important for the brain and body.
- Good sleep improves focus, memory, and thinking.
- Lack of sleep increases stress and errors.

## Question 5:
**What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?**

**Ans:**
- Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
- Avoid mobile and screens before sleeping.
- Do not drink caffeine like coffee at night.
- Keep the room dark, quiet, and cool.
- Relax before bed (music, reading).

## Question 6:
**Paraphrase the video - Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise. Minimum 5 points, only the points.**

**Ans:**
- Exercise improves brain focus and attention.
- It helps reduce stress and anxiety.
- Regular exercise improves memory and learning.
- It makes you feel happier.
- It improves sleep quality.

## Question 7:
**What are some steps you can take to exercise more?**

**Ans:**
- Set a fixed time daily for exercise.
- Start with short workouts.
- Walk instead of using vehicles when possible.
- Increase number of sets of exercise weekly for improvement.
- Exercise with a friend for motivation.
